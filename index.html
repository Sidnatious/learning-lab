Our programs target youth ages 6-18 to learn about science experiments and computer programming

We aim to provide a space for young girls and women to improve their study and mastery of the maths and sciences through tutoring programs and interactive education modules outside of traditional classroom settings.

Group Outings

Students go on group outings that are both fun and educational, such a trip to the beach to study the physics of waves, or a visit to nearby universities to be inspired to continue on to higher education

Weekly Clubs

Our goal is to make science fun! Our projects make physics and chemistry jump off of the blackboard and into our daily lives. We focus on using sustainable, locally available materials so students can continue study at home with their parents or friends.

School Events

We organize large scale science exploration days at local schools. Typically between 30-50 girls can spend all day rotating through experiments relating to physics, chemistry, and biology. They also will have the opportunity to gain insight to working in scientific fields from female professionals.
